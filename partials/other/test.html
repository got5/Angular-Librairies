• AngularJS is a great framework for testing all the functionalities of your application.
  • It provides Unit testing and End-to-End (E2E) testing. Both have different purposes:
    • Unit testing: Isolates and tests small "units" of code, without taking context into consideration.
    • E2E testing: Runs and tests full areas of an application, from the beginning to the end. Generally, they reproduce typical user operations.
