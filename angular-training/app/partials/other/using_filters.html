• Angular filters are used to format data for display to the user.
  • They are especially used to format binding expressions in your templates.
  • Filters can be chained, and can also take optional arguments.
<div ng-init="unfilteredPrice = '27.95'"> Price: {{unfilteredPrice}} <br/> Price: {{unfilteredPrice | currency}} <br/> Price: {{unfilteredPrice | number:5}} <br/> Price: {{unfilteredPrice | number:0 | currency}} <br/> Price: {{unfilteredPrice | currency | number:4}} <br/> </div>
