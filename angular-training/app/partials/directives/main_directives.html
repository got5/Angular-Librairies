• ngApp: Designates the root of the application, generally placed at the root of the page. Only one per HTML document.
  • ngController: Assigns behavior to a scope. Links a view to a controller.
  • ngInit: Specifies initialization tasks.
  • ngModel: Does two-way data binding between a $scope property and and an input/select/textarea or custom HTML component.
