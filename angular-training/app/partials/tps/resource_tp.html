• In the home page of our ecommerce application, we will display all the news added by the webmasters. Use the $resource service to manage news. Following functionalities are needed:
    1. List all the news in the table element.
    2. Displays the news of the day in the bottom panel. In our case, the selected news will be the one whose id is equals to 1.
    3. You can "like" a news, which will increase the value of its likes property.
    4. Delete a news.
    5. Add a news (news whose id is -1 are considered as newly created news).

    Use the url /news/:id with your $resource object.
